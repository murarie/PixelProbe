Become the Pixel Detective

Meet PixelProbe — your screen's personal detective! Cycle through solid colors, hunt down stubborn pixels, and see if your display is truly flawless.

Instructions

  • 1. Maximize your browser window or press F11 to enter full-screen mode.
  • 2. Hit the button below to launch the pixel test.
  • 3. Click anywhere on the screen to cycle through the test colors.
  • 4. Look closely for any pixels that refuse to change color — those might be stuck or dead.
  • 5. Bonus tip: Don't panic if you find one... That's just your monitor's birthmark.
